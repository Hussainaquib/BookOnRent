Who We Are

Our Story

Welcome to BookOnRent! We are a group of four friends living in different corners of India—Kolkata, Raipur, Delhi, and Mumbai. We realized that buying every book you want to read isn't always practical, whether due to budget constraints or lack of shelf space.

Our platform connects book lovers with our personal collections. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a casual reader, we are here to support your reading habit without breaking the bank. ☺️

Terms & Conditions

  • Rental Duration: Minimum rental period is 1 week.
  • Book Care: Please treat books with love. No writing, highlighting, or dog-earing pages.
  • Returns: Books must be returned in the same condition as issued.
  • Pricing: Prices are dynamic based on book demand and edition.
